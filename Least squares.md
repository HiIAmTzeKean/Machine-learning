# Least squares

To find the line of best fit that represents a [[Predictor]] and [[Response]].
## Idea
Finding [[Coefficient]]s that minimises the [[Residual sum of squares]].
### Equation
$$RSS = \sum_{i=1}^n (y_i - \beta_0 - \sum_{j=1}^p \beta_jX_{ij})^2$$
