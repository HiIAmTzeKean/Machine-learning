# Harmonic mean
One of several ways of computing [[Mean]]. Appropriate when **average rate** is of interest.
Expressed as the reciprocal of [[Arithmetic mean]] of the reciprocal of observations.
$$\text{Harmonic mean}=n\sum{\frac{1}{x_{i}}}$$
